When enabled, your playback speed preference will be saved and applied to all videos.

Current Playback Rate

Normal speed is 1.0. Recommended range: 0.125 to 4.0. Values outside this range may not work or may have no audio.

How much the speed changes when using hotkeys. Recommended: 0.125 or 0.25. Using multiples of 0.25 will show a checkmark in the native speed menu.

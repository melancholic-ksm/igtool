üîä Volume Settings

Remember Volume Level

When enabled, your volume preference will be saved and applied to all videos, including videos currently playing on the same page.

Current Volume Level

Set the volume level (0 = muted, 1 = 100%). You can also adjust using the video controls or hotkeys.

Volume Adjustment Step

How much the volume changes when using hotkeys. Recommended: 0.1 or 0.2

‚è© Playback Speed Settings

Remember Playback Rate

When enabled, your playback speed preference will be saved and applied to all videos.

Current Playback Rate

Normal speed is 1.0. Recommended range: 0.125 to 4.0. Values outside this range may not work or may have no audio.

Playback Rate Step

How much the speed changes when using hotkeys. Recommended: 0.125 or 0.25. Using multiples of 0.25 will show a checkmark in the native speed menu.
